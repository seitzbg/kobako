CHIE is made from 100% natural fragrance ingredients. Each raw material has been carefully selected and sourced from "Trees of Gods" found all around the world. Agarwood from Indonesia, Palo Santo from Peru, Hinoki from Japan, etc.

These "Trees of Gods" are fragrant trees that have been used for prayer, purification, and wisdom since ancient times. Developed to fit our daily lifestyles, CHIE has been brought to you from nature and conveniently packed into a 30 stick box.

The word "CHIE" means the ability to see things as they really are and to recognize the truth. Keeping true to the purity of this ancient wisdom, Chie does not use synthetic oils and is made only with natural aromatic ingredients in powder form. In order to make the incense naturally, each product's burning time has not been altered or adjusted, preserving its natural qualities and uniqueness.

Immerse yourself in the worldly ancient wisdom that flows from each stick. 

Connect with nature. Create space in your mind.

Agarwood: Harvested in Indonesia, Made in Japan. 
Agarwood is the damaged part of a tree that has turned into resin over a long period of time. It is characterized by a honey-like sweetness with the warmth of wood.

Discover

Chië (Chie)

This line is a collection of genuine incense made from natural aromatic ingredients.

Each fragrance comes from a different part of the world: Sandalwood from Australia, Agarwood from Indonesia, Palo Santo from Peru... there are 9 fragrances to enjoy in this collection.

Close your eyes and take a journey to each country, taking in the ancient wisdom that flows through each stick.

About Logo

Inspired by Buddhism, the Chie circle is painted with a single stroke in Zen calligraphy.

It is said that the circle represents the infinite, the beginning and end of everything, enlightenment, psychology, and the universe as a whole.

"Chië (Chie)" means the ability to see things as they really are and to recognize the truth.

Developed for modern-day lifestyles, yet keeping true to ancient wisdom, Chie does not use synthetic oils and is made only with natural aromatic ingredients in powder form.

In order to make the incense naturally, each product's burning time has not been altered or adjusted, preserving its natural qualities and uniqueness.

The packaging of the Chië collection is environmentally friendly as FSC (Forest Stewardship Coundil) certified paper is used, in which includes a naturally derived anti-mold agent.

Each package includes 30 sticks and a ceramic holder that is made in Seto, a kiln with a history of about 100 years.

In order to maintain the natural qualities and uniqueness for each incense, the burning time for each fragrance varies, and can be anywhere between 45 minutes to 70 minutes.

Chië Incense Holder - Brown

Chië also offers a light brown incense plate made by Hizen Yoshida Pottery from Ureshino City in Saga, Japan, known for its beautiful design using simple lines and traditional techniques that are preserved for generations.
